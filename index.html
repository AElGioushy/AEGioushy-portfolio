About Me

Ahmed Yasser Ahmed ElGioushy is a graduate of Legal Studies and International Relations from Pharos University in Alexandria and holds a Master’s Degree in International Transactions and Logistics from the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport (AASTMT). His areas of interest include governance, digital transformation, artificial intelligence, legal technology, and sustainability. He also completed certifications in AI and Governance & Digital Transformation through programs delivered by Engineers for Sustainable Egypt Foundation and NTI.
