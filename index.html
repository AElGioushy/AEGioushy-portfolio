About Me

I am Ahmed Yasser Ahmed ElGioushy, a Legal Studies and International Relations graduate from Pharos University in Alexandria, holding a Master’s Degree in International Transactions and Logistics from the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport (AASTMT). I specialize in governance, digital transformation, artificial intelligence, legal technology, and sustainability. As an AI Ambassador and Governance & Digital Transformation Ambassador, I am passionate about integrating law, innovation, and technology to support smart governance systems, sustainable institutional development, and digital transformation initiatives across Egypt and the MENA region.
