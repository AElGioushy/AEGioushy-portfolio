About Me

I am Ahmed Yasser Ahmed ElGioushy, a Legal Studies and International Relations graduate from Pharos University in Alexandria, holding a Master’s Degree in International Transactions and Logistics from the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport (AASTMT). My professional interests focus on governance, digital transformation, legal technology, artificial intelligence, sustainability, and international commercial relations. I am passionate about integrating law, technology, and innovation to support digital governance, smart legal systems, and sustainable institutional development in Egypt and the MENA region.
